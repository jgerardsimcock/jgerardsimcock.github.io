Since the UDacity course does not start until 12 December I decided to finish watching all the videos from 2nd Skies Forex. I also found a new tool; new for me at least. Trading View seems to aggregate the data feeds from a number of brokers and allows you to use their data and charting tools. Its a pretty great tool. You can even link your brokerage accounts so you can trade directly from the platform. Pretty great stuff. Here are some instruments I am watching.

Gold

I am watching gold because I have a long physical position in it. I’d like to better understand how I can possible hedge that position. It’s a bit of a strange idea since physical is my hedge against the movements of other financial assets.

EURO/USD

I am watching the Euro/Dollar because it is one of the most liquid instruments in the world.

USD/JPY

Same goes for the USD/JPY. Here I have it on two time contexts so that I can see if the lower time-frames give more clarity on the current trade opportunities.

USD/JPY Daily

USD/JPY 1 Hour

USD/RUS

My interest in USD/Russian Ruble is really about having some proxy to understand Russia. I am not sure this is a good one. Perhaps some kind of Russia ETF would do that. RSX, RSXJ, ERUS, and RUSL will be next to look at.

USD/MXN

USD/Peso interests me mostly because I am interested in Mexico and how it can differentiate itself in the world beyond simply being the neighbor of the USA. Maybe an ETF is another way to track and understand Mexico. EWW, MEXX, FLMX, HEWW could be worth a look.

My only current position is long physical Gold.
