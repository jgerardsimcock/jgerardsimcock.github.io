December 23, 2018

Your own thoughts

Agency, competence, sovereignty, independence, self-sufficiency, freedom. A bunch of words. Abstractions that point to something outside of you. Defined in some book or by some other human. Seemingly common short-cuts we use to transact with other humans and ourself. I’m concerned when I use these abstractions with myself to compel behavior. It’s actually not just these words. Its almost many words for many different behaviors and outcomes. Why do I need words and abstractions to marshall my resources to ends that I find meaningful? Why not see it and act until the vision is realized?

I am addicted to other peoples ideas and thoughts.

I have become dependent on their thoughts and their organizaton of reality to organize my own. Other people’s thoughts, other people’s lives, other people’s imagination, other people’s motivation, everything that is not me.

To be fair its more habit than addiction. However, from the expression of the energy is the same.

Why cannot I not see the end and act, fail, reflect, and act again until I achieve the goal? It’s an open question. Why do I need to know what another person did or thought or claims to have done or thought? A kind of very subtle cowardice and a lack of self-confidence that I can do, fail,reflect and do again and still believe in the process of doing, failing,reflecting and doing again.

The belief beyond doubt in the meta-loop is there for some activities. Just not for everything. The belief in the meta-loop exists. I guess for new, budding, hopeful activities, the belief in the loop is still weak.

I wanted to make it stronger so I can have real freedom, independence and agency.
