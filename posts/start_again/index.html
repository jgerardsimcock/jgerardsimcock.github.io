Start Again

In the twilight of my temporal youth, I decideded to start over. In some ways, I feel that my life has just started.

When your mind is consumed with something, not doing/living that thing is a kind of painful dishonesty. My relationship with pain is perhaps different than most folks. I don’t like pain that leads to regrets. I welcome pain that leads to freedom, awareness and peace.

So enough bullshit.

In late October 2018, I resigned from my job and decided to refocus my life. I was living in Hawaii, earning good money, with lots of autonomy, and a very high quality of life.

But the world was calling and there were parts of myself that I needed to develop and explore and bring into existence. I’ve been peering from the outside into financial markets since I was in University. For one reason or another, I never felt it was right to participate. There were always ‘more important’ things to do. When that statement no longer held true for me, there was only one option left.

It is very liberating state to know that there is nothing else you want to do. To spend my time thinking about markets, investing and growing wealth finally feels right.

The approach I’ve taken is a bit unorthodox. In some ways, this is the only way I know to do things. It’s raw, clumsy, and ungraceful at first. I’ve relocated to Bali to preserve capital, continue to surf and train. Also as Bali is a hub for digital nomads I wanted to see first hand what their version of reality and its future was.

I’ve been on the ground for a few days now. I’m working from Dojo Bali and training at Bali MMA. I wanted my life to be simple. I want to travel no more than 10-15 minutes to the place where I will work or train. So I am living in Canggu and so far I am stoked. There is even a good couple of surf breaks within 100 meters or so of Dojo Bali so I can get in dawn patrol session, shower and start working. Seems like a good start.

What I am doing all day is learning about markets, trading and how to use computers to trade. I’ve signed up for Udacity’s Artificial Intelligence for Trading Nanodegree. It begins next week. This week I have been busy going over linear algebra. Its been a few years and linalg always is a challenge for me.

I don’t plan to post code here. I only plan to post links to repositories or projects I am hosting elsewhere. The purpose of this will be to reflect and share the fruits of the process.
